Load Inventory

Quick Start:
  1. Make sure your Google Sheet has columns: SKU | PRODUCT Name | Quantity
  2. Share your Google Sheet: Click "Share" → "Anyone with the link can view"
  3. Enter the sheet name and range below (e.g., Sheet1!A1:C100)
  4. Click "Load Inventory" to begin
⚠️ Troubleshooting:
• Sheet must be publicly viewable
• Range must include headers in row 1
• Check browser console (F12) for errors
Format: SheetName!StartCell:EndCell (e.g., Sheet1!A1:C100)
